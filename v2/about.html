Welcome!

My name is Manuel, and I am (primarily) a front-end web developer. I started on my programming journey over a year ago by first diving into Python. After finishing the "Learn Python the Hard Way" course, I delved into Harvard's online CS50 course, which I found fascinating! From there, I transitioned into web development. I started off by learning HTML and CSS, then moved onto Javascript and jQuery. Currently, I am expanding my front-end skillset, while learning full-stack development (MEAN stack).

A bit more about me and my background - I was born in Bulgaria, but raised in the States. After graduating from Florida Atlantic University in May 2015, I traveled abroad to southeastern Asia where I spent about 6 months working and traveling. Although my degree is in Biosciences, I have always had a passion for tech. Stumbling upon programming around the end of 2015, I felt that I had found my niche.
